Founded in the early 2000s, the Gado-Gado Indonesian Students Association of UBC (GISAU) exists to create an open, close-knitted and connected community that exemplifies the signature Indonesian warmth and welcomes the diverse UBC society for Indonesian students in the UBC Vancouver campus
Our ultimate mission is to create a community that embodies the Indonesian warmth. With GISAU, you will get many opportunities to meet people through events, study sessions and hangouts. Who knows, maybe your next best friend is right here!

SOCIAL + FOOD

Varying from food, parties, ski trips, and sports, GISAU provides the most fun packed into one event. Wide range of experience as we also collab events with other Southeast Asian clubs! Check out our monthly social events on media pages [Tag us at @gisaubc to be featured at our Instagram page!]

RANTANGAN

Thousands of miles away from home and missing the food? Don’t worry, we got you covered with delicious, home-cooked Indonesian food brought to the Nest once every 2 weeks. Discover our past menu here and we are always open to suggestions for our next menu.
